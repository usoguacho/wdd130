My Favorite City

Las Vegas would be my favorite city because it has become a true sports hub, with teams like the Raiders, Golden Knights, and Aces bringing major league energy year-round. It’s also packed with family-friendly activities, from water parks and hiking at Red Rock Canyon to arcades, shows, and unique attractions on and off the Strip. And while the summers are hot, the dry climate means sunshine almost every day and mild winters that make it easy to stay active all year.
