Wild River Rafting Co.

At Wild River Rafting Co., our mission is to connect people with the raw, untamed beauty of nature through the thrill of whitewater rafting. We believe every person deserves an adventure that challenges their limits, fills their lungs with fresh air, and leaves them with memories that last a lifetime. Safety, excitement, and community are the currents that drive everything we do.

History

Wild River Rafting Co. was founded in 1998 by a group of river-obsessed adventurers who believed the best way to live was wet, loud, and fully present. What started as a single raft and a dream quickly grew into one of the region's most trusted rafting outfitters. Over the decades, we've navigated countless rapids, welcomed thousands of riders, and built a legacy rooted in passion for the river.

From humble beginnings on a single stretch of river, we've grown to offer expeditions across some of the most breathtaking waterways in the region. Our guides bring decades of combined experience, and our commitment to safety and adventure has never wavered. Every rapid we run is a tribute to the spirit that started it all — a love for the river and the people who dare to ride it.
