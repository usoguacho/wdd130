USA

The United States is a vast and diverse nation spanning from the Atlantic to the Pacific, made up of 50 states with a population of roughly 340 million people. Founded on principles of liberty and self-governance, it operates as a federal constitutional republic and has grown into the world's largest economy. Its landscape ranges from Arctic tundra in Alaska to tropical beaches in Hawaii, with everything in between — mountains, deserts, plains, and dense forests. Culturally, it is one of the most pluralistic societies on earth, shaped by centuries of immigration and a tradition of reinvention that continues to define the American experience.
